Welcome to the about section of the application

Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? Luckily for you, in the information age, there are open APIs to retrieve information about it and the app based on one of the APIs with full power of android system on it 🙂

Features

  • Find the places that you care about and they will always be next to you
  • View accurate and useful weather forecast for selected place
  • Saved information will be always with you regardless of internet connection

Guide

1. Cities Screen

On this screen you will see the list of places you have added before. Place can be easily removed by pressing the icon on the right of each item.

You can add new place by pressing the button on the bottom right of the screen.



2. Add City Screen

Here you can select a new place to add with full power of Google Places API. You can either select a place on the map, pick one of the places nearby or search for the location that you want to add.



3. Weather Screen

Weather forecast will be displayed on this screen for the city that you want.
